# SEO Strategy for Edexo ERP Website

**Date:** January 24, 2026  
**Website:** Edexo ERP - Education & Business Management Suite  
**Target Market:** India (Schools, Coaching Centers, Educational Institutions, Businesses)

## 📋 Executive Summary

This comprehensive SEO strategy is designed to help Edexo ERP rank higher in Google search results and attract more qualified leads from educational institutions and businesses across India. The strategy focuses on technical optimization, content marketing, local SEO, and link building to establish Edexo as a leading ERP solution provider.

## 🚀 Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Technical SEO Foundation (Weeks 1-2)

#### 1.1 Create robots.txt
```txt
User-agent: *
Allow: /
Sitemap: https://edexoerp.com/sitemap.xml

# Block admin and sensitive areas
Disallow: /admin/
Disallow: /wp-admin/
Disallow: /cgi-bin/
```

#### 1.2 Generate sitemap.xml
- Create XML sitemap with all important pages
- Include blog posts, landing pages, and service pages
- Submit to Google Search Console

#### 1.3 Add Meta Tags to index.html
```html
<meta name="description" content="Edexo ERP - Complete education and business management software for schools, coaching centers, and enterprises in India. AI-powered, affordable, and easy to use.">
<meta name="keywords" content="ERP software, school management, education software, institute management, coaching center software, business ERP India">
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
<meta name="author" content="Edexo ERP">
<meta name="robots" content="index, follow">
```

#### 1.4 Implement Structured Data
Add JSON-LD schema markup for:
- SoftwareApplication
- Organization
- LocalBusiness (for Indian offices)
- Product reviews and ratings

#### 1.5 Open Graph and Twitter Cards
```html
<meta property="og:title" content="Edexo ERP - Future of Institutional & Enterprise Management">
<meta property="og:description" content="Transform your institution with our AI-powered ERP solution designed for Indian schools and businesses.">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://edexoerp.com/social-preview.jpg">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://edexoerp.com">
<meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image">
<meta name="twitter:site" content="@edexoerp">
```

#### 1.6 Image Optimization
- Add descriptive alt text to all images
- Compress images for faster loading
- Use descriptive file names (e.g., "school-erp-dashboard.jpg")
